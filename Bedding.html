How to choose the perfect bedding for a comfortable sleep




Bed linen is an integral part of any bedroom, which plays a key role in creating comfortable conditions for sleep and relaxation. Choosing the right bedding can significantly improve your sleep quality, improve your mood, and even impact your overall health. In this article, we will look at the main aspects to consider when choosing bed linen, and also discuss which materials and designs are most popular in modern interiors.

The importance of fabric density

Fabric density, measured in threads per square inch (threads/cm²), is an important indicator of bedding quality. The higher the density, the softer and more durable the linen will be. For example, a density of 200-300 threads/cm² is considered a good option for everyday use, while 400-600 threads/cm² is considered more premium.

Bedding materials The first and most important aspect of choosing bedding is the material. It directly affects the comfort and durability of the product. The most popular materials include:

Cotton

This is a classic choice for bedding. Cotton underwear is soft, breathable and durable. Satin and percale are varieties of cotton that have a smooth texture and luxurious shine.

Design and color palette

Bed linen design is not only a matter of aesthetics, but also a way to create a certain mood in the bedroom. Calming pastel shades such as blue, beige or gray promote relaxation and improve sleep quality. Bright, bold colors like red or orange can add energy, but are best used in small accents.

It is also important to consider the overall style of the interior. For modern minimalist interiors, single-color sets with a minimalist design are suitable. For classic bedrooms, it is better to choose linen with ornaments or floral patterns.
