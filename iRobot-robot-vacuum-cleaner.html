What is iRobot?

iRobot is an American company specializing in the development and production of robots for various purposes. Founded in 1990 by a group of robotics experts from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, the company initially focused on creating robots for military applications. However, iRobot later shifted its focus to consumer products and introduced the first Roomba vacuum robot in 2002, which quickly gained popularity among consumers.

Intelligent Navigation: iRobot devices are equipped with advanced navigation systems, such as the iAdapt technology, which allows the robot to scan the room and create a map for efficient movement. The robot avoids obstacles, does not fall down stairs, and thoroughly cleans the entire area.

Powerful Suction: iRobot devices feature powerful motors that provide excellent suction, allowing them to effectively remove dust, dirt, and pet hair from all types of flooring, whether carpets or hard floors.

Three-Stage Cleaning System: Most iRobot models use a three-stage cleaning system, which includes side brushes for cleaning along walls and corners, a main brush for lifting dirt, and powerful suction for removal.

Automatic Recharge: After completing a cleaning session or when the battery runs low, the robot automatically returns to its charging station, ensuring uninterrupted operation.

Programming and App Control: Modern iRobot models support mobile app control. Users can schedule cleaning times, select modes, and even control the device remotely while away from home.

Smart Sensors: iRobot devices are equipped with numerous sensors that help them detect pollution levels, avoid collisions with furniture, and recognize different surfaces to automatically adjust their cleaning mode.

How to Choose an iRobot Vacuum Robot?

When choosing an iRobot vacuum robot, consider several factors:

Cleaning Area: For larger homes and spaces, choose models with advanced navigation and longer battery life.

Type of Flooring: If you have carpets in your home, look for models with strong suction and specialized brushes for carpets.

Pet Ownership: If you have pets, select models designed specifically for picking up pet hair and effective cleaning.

Additional Features: If features like app control or scheduling are important to you, look for more modern models that support these technologies.
